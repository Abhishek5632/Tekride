Multiple Pickup / Drop Locations

How It Works

Shared Pickup/Drop Locations: For a quick and coordinated journey, choose the same pickup or drop-off point for everyone.

Individual Pickup/Drop Locations: Traveling from different locations? No problem! Specify unique pickup and drop-off points for each passenger.

Why It’s Convenient

  • Flexibility: Perfect for groups with varying schedules and routes.
  • Time-Saving: Avoid detours and delays by organizing individual stops.
  • Seamless Experience: Enjoy a stress-free ride with clear and accurate location details.

Who Benefits from This Feature?

  • Families: Pickup and drop off at multiple points for added convenience.
  • Friends: Join the ride from different locations without hassle.
  • Colleagues: Efficiently coordinate office pickups and drop-offs.
